Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 9, 2025

This privacy policy governs the Home Assistant Alexa Skill integration for personal use. It describes how data is handled during account linking and smart home control.

Information We Collect

  • Account Linking Data: User ID and basic profile info (e.g., email) from Amazon to link your Alexa account to Home Assistant. This is used solely for authentication.
  • Smart Home Data: Commands and status for exposed entities (e.g., lights, switches) in Home Assistant. No personal data is stored beyond what's needed for device control.
  • No Additional Collection: No voice recordings, locations, or unrelated data is collected. All processing happens on your local Home Assistant instance.

How We Use Data

Data is used only to enable voice control of your Home Assistant devices via Alexa. It is not shared with third parties.

Security

Your data is protected via HTTPS and OAuth. Access can be revoked anytime in the Alexa app or Amazon account settings.

Your Rights

You can unlink accounts in the Alexa app (More > Skills & Games > [Your Skill] > Settings > Disconnect). Contact Amazon support for data deletion requests.

Changes

This policy may be updated; check back for changes.

For questions, refer to Home Assistant's official privacy policy or Amazon's privacy notice.
